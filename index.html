Cactus

  • Cacti are unique and interesting plants that are relatively easy to care for, once you understand their basic needs.

  • Cacti don't require frequent watering. In fact, overwatering can be harmful to them

  • Cacti need plenty of sunlight to grow. Place your cactus in a south-facing window or under grow lights if you don't have enough natural light.

  • Cacti need well-draining soil to prevent root rot. You can buy cactus mix at a garden center, or make your own by mixing equal parts potting soil, perlite, and coarse sand.

  • Cacti don't need much fertilizer, but they do benefit from a light feeding during the growing season. Use a cactus fertilizer or a balanced fertilizer diluted to half strength.
